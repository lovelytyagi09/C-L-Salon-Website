🌸 Aftercare

For the first 72 hours, avoid washing, tying or tucking hair behind your ears. Use sulfate-free shampoo and a smoothing conditioner. Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant when you do. Sharon will guide you through a full aftercare routine after your treatment.
AftercareHair Care

We recommend colour-safe, sulfate-free shampoos and a weekly nourishing mask to maintain vibrancy. Avoid washing with very hot water as this strips colour faster. Sharon can recommend specific products suited to your hair at your appointment.
