What is the Private Room?

C L Salon's private room is Birmingham's first fully enclosed private salon space — built specifically for Hijabi women who deserve world-class hair care without compromising their modesty or comfort.

When Sharon saw that Muslim women in Birmingham had limited access to quality hair services that respected their values, she didn't add a curtain or a screen. She built a room. A real, enclosed, private space with its own door — where everything that happens in the main salon happens in complete privacy.

The private room is available every day. All staff present during your appointment are female. Every service we offer — Japanese straightening, Korean perms, colour, cuts, treatments — is fully available inside.

There is no additional charge for using the private room. It is simply part of how we welcome every woman.

Questions

Private Room FAQs

Is it truly fully enclosed — not just a curtain?
Yes. The private room at C L Salon is a real, fully enclosed room with its own door — completely separate from the main salon floor. It is not a curtained area or a partition. Nobody in the salon can see inside while you are there.
Will there be any male staff present?
No. Throughout your entire appointment in the private room, only female staff are present. This is our absolute commitment — there are no exceptions to this policy. You are welcome to confirm this when booking with Sharon.
Can I get Japanese straightening or colour in the private room?
Yes — every single service we offer is available in the private room. Japanese straightening, Korean perms, digital perms, full colour, balayage, haircuts, blow drys, hair treatments — all fully available with no exclusions.
Is there an extra charge for the private room?
No. There is absolutely no surcharge for using the private room. You pay the same as any other client. The private room is simply part of how we welcome every woman at C L Salon.
How do I request the private room when booking?
Simply WhatsApp Sharon and tell her which service you'd like and that you want the private room. She'll confirm availability and arrange everything for you personally. The private room is available every day of the week.
Do I need to come in for a consultation first?
For specialist treatments like Japanese straightening, perms and colour, a consultation is recommended — and this can also take place in the private room. For simpler services like haircuts and blow drys, you can book directly. Sharon is happy to advise via WhatsApp before you visit.
