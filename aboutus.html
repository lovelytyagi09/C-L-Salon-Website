Our Heritage

Specialist Asian Hair Styling
Brought to Birmingham

Sharon's training spans decades across Hong Kong and Malaysia — the epicentres of Asian hair technique. She didn't learn about Asian hair styling from a textbook. She trained in the salons that pioneered the methods.

Asian hair has unique properties — thickness, density, natural weight, and specific responses to heat and chemical treatments. Most Western-trained stylists work around these. Sharon works with them.

Japanese hair straightening here uses authentic Japanese products and processes — not adapted Western equivalents. Korean digital perms are executed with the precision the technique demands. Colour work is formulated for your actual hair, not a general guide.

This heritage matters for every client — not just Asian women. Sharon's depth of technical understanding means she reads every head of hair with the same precision, the same care, the same standard. The only specialist Asian hair salon in Birmingham Chinatown — and the only one with a private room for Hijabi women.
