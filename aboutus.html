Sharon

Sharon is the heartbeat of C L Salon 名髮廊. Born from a life dedicated to the craft, she brought over 40 years of Hong Kong and Malaysian hair artistry to the heart of Birmingham's Chinatown — and changed what a salon could mean for women in this city.

Her mission was never just about beautiful hair. It was about belonging. Sharon noticed a gap — Hijabi women, Asian women, women of every background — who wanted world-class hair care but found no salon that truly welcomed them. So she built one.

Trained across Asia in the most demanding disciplines — Japanese straightening, Korean perms, precision colour, advanced treatments — Sharon brings a level of skill that most stylists never reach. Every appointment is personal. Every result is considered.

Today, C L Salon carries Sharon's philosophy in every corner: no compromise on privacy, no rush, no judgement. Just exceptional hair, in a space built for you.

Our Heritage

Asian Hair Artistry
Brought to Birmingham

Sharon's training spans decades across Hong Kong and Malaysia — the epicentres of Asian hair technique. She didn't learn about Asian hair from a textbook. She trained in the salons that pioneered the methods.

Asian hair has unique properties — thickness, density, natural weight, and specific responses to heat and chemical treatments. Most Western-trained stylists work around these. Sharon works with them.

Japanese hair straightening here uses authentic Japanese products and processes — not adapted Western equivalents. Korean digital perms are executed with the precision the technique demands. Colour work is formulated for your actual hair, not a general guide.

This heritage matters for every client — not just Asian women. Sharon's depth of technical understanding means she reads every head of hair with the same precision, the same care, the same standard.
