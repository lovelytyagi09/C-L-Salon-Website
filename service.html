Questions

Common Questions

Do I need to book a separate consultation?
No — just book your appointment directly. Sharon will carry out your consultation at the start of your visit before any treatment begins. There is no need for a separate consultation booking.
How long do Japanese straightening results last?
Japanese straightening is permanent — your treated hair will remain straight as it grows. You will need touch-ups as new growth comes in, typically every 6–12 months. The treatment grows out beautifully with no harsh line of demarcation.
Can I get any service in the private room?
Yes — all of our services are available in the private room upstairs with no exception. Colour, perms, straightening, haircuts — all fully accessible. Simply request the private room when booking via WhatsApp. There is no surcharge. Please note the private room is closed on Wednesdays.
What is the Collagen Hair Streamer?
The Collagen Hair Streamer is a smoothing and shining treatment using collagen-infused steam. It softens and smooths the hair without permanently changing your texture — and adds a brilliant shine. It takes within the hour and is great as a standalone treatment or add-on.
Are you open every day?
We are open Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday — 10am to 6pm. We are closed every Wednesday.
How do I know which treatment is right for my hair?
Book your appointment and Sharon will assess your hair in person — condition, texture, history and goals — then recommend the ideal treatment before proceeding. No obligation to commit until you're happy with the plan.
What aftercare is needed after a perm or straightening?
Sharon will provide personalised aftercare advice at your appointment. Generally, avoid washing your hair for 48–72 hours, use sulphate-free shampoo, and avoid tight styles initially. She'll recommend specific products suited to your hair.
