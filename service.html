Questions

Common Questions

Do I need a consultation before booking?
A consultation is required before Japanese straightening, perms, colour and extensions. For simpler services like haircuts and blow drys you can book directly. When in doubt, book a free consultation first — it's always included for complex treatments.
How long do Japanese straightening results last?
Japanese straightening is permanent — your treated hair will remain straight as it grows. You will need touch-ups as new growth comes in, typically every 6–12 months. The treatment grows out beautifully with no harsh line of demarcation.
Can I get any service in the private room?
Yes — all of our services are available in the private room with no exception. Colour, perms, straightening, haircuts — all fully accessible. Simply request the private room when booking via WhatsApp or Fresha. There is no surcharge for using it.
How do I know which treatment is right for my hair?
Book a free consultation with Sharon. She'll assess your hair's condition, texture, history and goals — then recommend the ideal treatment. No pressure, no obligation.
What aftercare is needed after a perm or straightening?
Sharon will provide personalised aftercare advice at your appointment. Generally, avoid washing your hair for 48–72 hours, use sulphate-free shampoo, and avoid tight styles initially. She'll recommend specific products suited to your hair.
